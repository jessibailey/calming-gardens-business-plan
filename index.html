01 Executive Summary

A Garden Sanctuary for Healing

Nature-inspired massage therapy that supports mental and physical wellness for people of all ages and abilities.

Vision

A community where everyone can access a peaceful, nature-inspired space for healing, where touch, tranquility, and compassion support the mental and physical wellness of people of all ages and abilities.

Mission

Calming Gardens exists to support mental and physical wellness by combining massage therapy with nature. My mission is to help people of all ages, including pregnant clients and those with special needs or disabilities, feel safe, cared for, and deeply relaxed in a calm, garden-inspired space.

02 Services

Therapeutic Offerings

Customized sessions with aromatherapy and nature-inspired elements, from relaxation to specialized care.

Deep Tissue

Targeted pressure to release chronic tension and restore mobility in deep muscle layers.

Prenatal Massage

Side-lying technique designed for pregnant women — easing pain, swelling, and preparing the body for birth.

Hot Stone Therapy

Heated basalt stones combined with massage to deeply relax muscles and improve circulation.

Aromatherapy

Garden-grown herbal oils and blends integrated into sessions for enhanced relaxation and healing.

Sports Recovery

Pre-event, post-event, and maintenance massage for athletes — targeted deep tissue and stretching.

Adaptive / Special Needs

Gentle, trauma-informed, sensory-aware sessions for children and adults with disabilities.

Reflexology

Precise pressure on feet, hands, and ears to promote full-body healing and stress relief.

Geriatric Massage

Gentle, adaptive techniques for older adults focusing on circulation, comfort, and mobility.

Thai Bodywork

Mat-based assisted stretching and compression combining yoga-like movements with massage.

09 Growth Roadmap

The Path Forward

From shared studio launch to a fully independent garden wellness space with bath and sauna experiences.

First 6 Months

Foundation & Launch

Secure LMT license, set up shared studio in Crown Heights/Bed-Stuy with garden decor and essential equipment. Build savings, grow clientele to 20+ weekly sessions, and establish community of fellow massage therapists for support and referrals.

Year 1

Establish & Grow

Design the studio's signature garden identity. Build loyal repeat clientele through exceptional care and word-of-mouth. Begin expanding gardens for homemade product ingredients. Target 50% capacity with $72K annual revenue.

Year 2

Expand Offerings

Add portable infrared sauna. Grow to 75% capacity at $105K revenue. Expand retail product line with garden-grown oils, teas, and bee products. Scale paid advertising to $500/mo. Pay off microloan.

Year 3+

Full Garden Spa Vision

Expand into fuller garden-spa experience with baths and dedicated sauna. Reach 90% capacity at $129K revenue. Explore own studio space on own land — becoming a trusted, inclusive wellness destination in the community.
